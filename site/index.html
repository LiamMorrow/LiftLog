Features

Everything a lifter needs.
Nothing they don't.

Every feature exists because it makes tracking faster or more useful. Nothing else.

Auto rest timer

LiftLog starts your rest clock the moment you log a set, so you can put the phone down and focus on recovering.

Progress tracking

Charts that show how your lifts are trending over time. Spot plateaus before they kill your momentum.

Progressive overload

LiftLog tracks your weights and reps over time and automatically suggests when to add more load. That progression is the core of actually getting stronger.

Upcoming workouts

See what's on deck before you walk through the door. Never waste warm-up time figuring out the plan.

Fully offline

Your data is stored locally. No account or sync required. It works underground, in a dead zone, everywhere.

Open source

Every line of code is public on GitHub. Audit it, fork it, contribute to it. No black boxes.

Pick your workout

Choose from your saved programs or start fresh. LiftLog remembers your last session so you know exactly where to pick up.

Complete a set, tap the screen. The rest timer starts automatically. Adjust reps or weight on the fly if the plan needs tweaking.

03
See your progress

After each session, LiftLog updates your charts and flags any personal records. Your history is always a tap away.
